Who We Are

Competition Meets Community

The Northeastern Climbing Team is the official competitive rock climbing team sanctioned through Northeastern University Club Sports.

Elite Training

Train at Central Rock Gym locations across Massachusetts with 3-4 practices per week.

National Competition

Compete through USA Climbing's Collegiate Series at local, divisional, and national levels.

Strong Community

Join a tight-knit team that gives back through volunteer events and community outreach.

Discover Our Story
Our Mission
"The Northeastern University Club Climbing Team exists to provide resources to and empower climbers of all identities and backgrounds who are interested in the world of competition climbing."

We strive each year to train and perform at the local, regional, national, and international levels, while generating a positive impact on the stewardship of the greater New England climbing community.
