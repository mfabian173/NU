Diversity & Inclusion

Building a welcoming, equitable, and inclusive climbing community at Northeastern.

"We believe climbing is for everyone, and we are committed to fostering an environment where all climbers can thrive, regardless of their background, identity, or experience level."

Our Commitment

The Northeastern Climbing Team is dedicated to creating and maintaining an inclusive environment that celebrates diversity in all its forms. We recognize that the climbing community—both historically and presently—has not always been welcoming to people of all backgrounds. We are actively working to change that narrative, starting with our own team.

We believe that diverse perspectives strengthen our team, enhance our problem-solving abilities on the wall, and create a richer, more supportive community for everyone. Our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion is not just a statement—it's reflected in our actions, policies, and daily interactions.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Belonging

Every member of our team should feel valued, respected, and that they truly belong. We create spaces where everyone can bring their authentic selves.

Accessibility

We work to reduce barriers to participation in climbing, whether financial, physical, or social. Climbing should be accessible to all who want to try it.

Safety

Physical and emotional safety are paramount. We maintain zero tolerance for discrimination, harassment, or behavior that makes anyone feel unsafe.

Education

We commit to continuous learning about DEI issues and their impact on the climbing community. Education is an ongoing journey, not a destination.

Respect

We treat all individuals with dignity and respect, honoring different perspectives, experiences, and identities that each person brings to our community.

Advocacy

We actively advocate for equity in climbing spaces beyond our team, using our platform to promote positive change in the broader climbing community.

Taking Action

How We Put Values Into Practice

Inclusive Tryout Process

Our tryout process evaluates potential, attitude, and commitment—not just current climbing ability. We actively recruit climbers from diverse backgrounds and experience levels.

Financial Support

We work to ensure financial constraints don't prevent anyone from participating. We offer payment plans, scholarship opportunities, and fundraise to reduce member dues.

Community Outreach

We partner with local organizations to introduce climbing to underrepresented communities through intro clinics, gym visits, and mentorship programs.

Team Education

We hold regular discussions and workshops on DEI topics, creating space for learning, reflection, and growth as a team.

Leadership Representation

We strive for diverse representation in team leadership positions and actively mentor members from underrepresented backgrounds for leadership roles.

Feedback & Accountability

We maintain open channels for feedback and hold ourselves accountable for continuous improvement. We listen, learn, and adapt based on our members' experiences.
