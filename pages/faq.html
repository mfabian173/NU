Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Northeastern Climbing Team.

How many people are on the team?

Our team consists of 24 climbers, 12 women and 12 men.

How do I tryout?

Tryout details can be found on our Tryouts page. We host our main tryouts in the beginning of the fall semester and limited tryouts early in the spring semester to fill all roles.

Can graduate students tryout?

Yes, we love our grad student team members!

What format are tryouts?

We hold a redpoint-format bouldering competition where tryouts can participate. The scoring is based on your performance on competition-style boulder problems.

Where do you practice?

We practice at Central Rock Gym (CRG)'s gyms in Watertown, Cambridge, and Stoneham.

What competitions do you participate in?

Our team officially participates in USA Climbing Locals, Divisionals, and Nationals. Additionally, we attend other local competitions like the Dark Horse Series, the Movement Boulder Series, and the Blackout Boulder Brawl.

How often does the team practice?

We have 3-4 practices per week. The schedule includes full team practices on Sundays and Mondays, and half team practices on Wednesdays and Thursdays. Potential members should be prepared to attend at least 3 practices per week.

Is transportation provided to practices?

Yes! Transportation to practices is provided via the Northeastern Club Sports vans, departing from Renaissance Garage.

Do I need climbing experience to tryout?

While prior climbing experience is helpful, we welcome climbers of all skill levels to try out. It's a great opportunity to experience competition-style climbing and meet other passionate climbers, regardless of whether you make the team.

What's the time commitment?

Team members are expected to attend at least 3 practices per week, plus competitions throughout the season. Practices are typically 2-3 hours each. The team is understanding of academic commitments and co-ops.

Still Have Questions?

Don't see your question answered? Reach out to us directly and we'll be happy to help!
