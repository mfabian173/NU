Tryouts will be scored in a boulder redpoint format to fill in our roster spots for the Fall and Spring semesters.

Know Before You Go

General Information

Fall Tryouts

Full Roster Open

In the fall, all 24 of our roster spots (12 male, 12 female) are open to everybody trying out. Climbers will exclusively try out in a boulder competition format.

Spring Tryouts

Limited Spots

In the spring, we host limited tryouts only to fill roster spots of teammates who have graduated or are leaving for co-op or dialogue. These also feature a boulder competition format.

Questions?

Tryouts FAQ

If I make the team, do I have to join?

Absolutely not, although you would love it! üôÇ

Do I need to know how to belay?

Nope! Tryouts are boulder-only, so no belaying required.

Can I try out if I'm a grad student?

Yes! We love our grad student team members!

I'm not sure if I'll make it, should I bother trying out?

You should definitely try out! It's a great way to meet other enthusiastic climbers and an awesome opportunity to gain experience on competition-style climbs!

How do I get to the gym?

We provide transportation to fall tryouts via the Northeastern Club Sports Vans! When you register for tryouts you are reserving your spot in these vans. We'll be leaving from Renaissance Garage.
