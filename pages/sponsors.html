Become a Sponsor

If you are interested in becoming an official sponsor of the Northeastern Climbing Team, we'd love to hear from you!

As a sponsor, you'll be supporting a dedicated group of student-athletes who compete at the highest levels of collegiate climbing while maintaining academic excellence and giving back to the community.

Sponsorship Benefits:

  • ✓ Featured on our website and social media
  • ✓ Exposure at competitions and events
  • ✓ Association with a top collegiate climbing program
