Why Your Support Matters

As a club sport, the Northeastern Climbing Team receives limited university funding. Your donation directly supports our athletes and enables us to:

  • Cover competition entry fees for all team members
  • Fund travel to regional and national competitions
  • Provide gym memberships for team training
  • Purchase team gear, equipment, and uniforms
  • Host community events and intro clinics
❤️

Tax-Deductible Giving

Donations to Northeastern University Athletics are tax-deductible as allowed by law. You'll receive a receipt for your records.
